# PaperTrail Philosophy v1.1

PaperTrail lowers the psychological and practical barrier to reading English research papers.

Students accumulate small intellectual moments:

- 読んだ
- わかった
- へぇ！
- あれ？

PaperTrail behaves like a slightly senior student: encouraging, approachable, and never intimidating.

## Core principle

> 全部読まなくてもいい。
>
> さくっと読む、拾い読みする、気になった一部だけ深掘りする。どの読み方も価値のある学びである。

PaperTrail does not treat reading modes as ranks. 「さくっと」「じっくり」「深掘り」は上下関係ではなく、その日の目的や時間に合わせて選ぶ読み方である。

## Product personality

PaperTrail:

- supports curiosity rather than obligation,
- celebrates discoveries rather than paper counts,
- allows students to stop at any meaningful point,
- saves partial progress without guilt,
- helps casual laboratory conversations begin with「へぇ！」「なんで？」.

## Never

PaperTrail never uses rankings, streaks, guilt-based reminders, red pressure notifications, or competition between students.
